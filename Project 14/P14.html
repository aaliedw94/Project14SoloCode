Project 14 Display: Google Maps and current date and time

Welcome to my mini Google Maps project. Here are the few functionalities that were implemented for the project so far.

  • The current time and date are displayed based on your time zone.
  • My page header will both change to a random color every 10 seconds, and display a small message of the RGB value of the current color.
  • Clicking anywhere on the map will show the latitude and longitude in a pop up window.
  • Below the map, you can search for a location and the map will update to show the location with a dropped marker. Clicking the marker will provide a small welcome message.
  • The placed marker can be dragged around the map, once placed.
  • You can type in two locations, an origin and destination, which will show the quickest route based on transportation type and will display the travel time below the input as well.
  • Lastly, if you'd like to reset the map, just click the reset button below!
